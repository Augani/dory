How it compares

Free and open, on purpose.

Two things cost nothing to adopt and are hard to reverse: no license line item to buy or track, and an open-source codebase whose terms can't be quietly changed later. Everything below is sourced.

Permanently free & open source

GPL-3.0, no paid tier, no seat count, no account. That removes a per-seat license line item and the ongoing job of tracking who's entitled to a commercial seat when your headcount or revenue grows.

Terms you can't lose

Pricing and free-tier terms for closed-source tools have changed before, and some developers have said publicly they no longer trust closed-source freemium runtimes for that reason. An open GPL-3.0 codebase is a commitment that can't be revoked.
